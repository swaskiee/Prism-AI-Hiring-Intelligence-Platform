India Runs Hackathon · Track 1 · Redrob AI

One candidate pool.
Split into every signal that matters.

Prism doesn't rank candidates by keyword density. It separates 100,000 profiles into four independent, explainable signals — semantic fit, behavioral trust, structural red flags, and data-integrity anomalies — then recombines them into a shortlist a recruiter can actually trust and defend.

Semantic Fit — career history, not keywords
Behavioral Trust — response rate, recency
Structural Flags — title, tenure, domain checks
Anomaly Gate — honeypot exclusion
Why ranking this way

Redrob's own challenge brief names the trap directly: keyword density rewards the wrong candidates and filters out the right ones. Prism is built specifically against that failure mode.
